Who am I

I'm a master's student in Social Studies of Gender with the main research interest in gender and technology, and a newbie of the coding world who are eager to learn new things.

Technology is a medium of power.

– Cynthia Cockburn, Machinery of Dominance

To combine my interest in programming with my sociological research, I am currently working on a project focusing on immigrant female in Sweden who are learning to code.
It's a qualitative study which explores women's intersectional experience in the tech sector and the underrepresentation of women in this male-dominated field.
